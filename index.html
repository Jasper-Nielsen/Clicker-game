Story

Winner Winner Christmas Dinner You have just arrived at Christmas dinner with the family. Unbeknownst to you this year’s putlock feat theme is healthy foods. Everyone has brought healthy dishes except you. As the industrious person you are, you have made a whole Christmas dinner with three very non healthy courses. Your family making fun of you and berating you for bringing fatty unhealthy food, laugh at your contribution through the entire setting of the table. That’s until you all sit down. Everyone stares at your perfectly cooked goose with gravy and coated potatoes and all of your other dishes with longing. Everyone else has loaded their plates with healthy food stuff. But they are not eating, just pushing the food around on the plate. The attention and hypocrisy is palpable, as you take a knife and cut a piece of the goose. Complete silence all eyes follow the cut’s travel to your plate. Only thing to be heard is the meat as it hits your plate. Bang chairs falling over, cutlery clanking when all hell breaks loose as each person frantically reaches out for their desired food. A hand snatches the piece on your plate. Your brother shouting “you snooze you lose”. Filled with frustration you look around the room, watching as your parents each with their hands on the gravy boat, trying to wrestle it from the other, and your sister has planted her face in the rice pudding. Seeing no food easily accessible you picking up a piece something leafy green, and chews on it. Your brother has brought a sautéed piece of kale, a piece of fucking KALE!!. This will not stand. No one is going to laugh at your dinner, bring “delicious” healthy food eat your well prepared dinner and get away with it. Show them what you are made of. Eat your fill! Be vigilant: don’t let anyone take from your plate. Be quick, before you know it. All the good stuff will be gone.
